About

Flat is a flat-styled Bootstrap responsive navbar, presented in three different styles, that includes submenus and off canvas menu. Built with Sass, includes SCSS files for deep customization. Presented in three different styles and two color variations. Features missed components in Bootstrap 3, multilevel submenus and off-canvas navigation. Completed with examples and documentation.
